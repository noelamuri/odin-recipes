Lasagna

Preparation guidelines

This is a special homemade lasagna recipe with a meaty, made-from-scratch tomato sauce and a deliciously cheesy filling.

Ingredients

  • Meat, onion, canned tomatoes and fresh herbs
  • Sugar, spices and seasonings
  • Noodles, cheese and eggs

Steps

  1. Cook meat with onion.
  2. Boil and drain noodles.
  3. Mix ricotta, mozzarella, and eggs.
  4. Layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheese mixture.
  5. Bake at 375°F (190°C) covered for 30 minutes, then uncovered for 15 minutes.
  6. Serve and enjoy your savory lasagna.
