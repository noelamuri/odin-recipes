Grilled cheese sandwich

Preparation guidelines

Enjoy a classic grilled cheese sandwich, perfect for a quick and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • Bread slices
  • Cheese slices
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Heat a skillet or griddle over medium heat.
  2. Butter one side of each bread slice.
  3. Place a cheese slice between two bread slices, buttered sides facing out.
  4. Place the sandwich in the skillet and cook until golden brown on both sides and the cheese is melted.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious grilled cheese sandwich.
